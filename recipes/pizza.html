Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Ground pork

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles for 10 minutes. They do not need to be fully cooked. Set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  3. Cook the meats together. Drain.
  4. Add the sauce to the meat.
  5. In 13x9 pan, put sauce on the bottom layer.
  6. Put a layer of ricotta cheese on top of the sauce.
  7. Put a layer of lasagna noodles on top of cheese.
  8. Sprinkle mozarella cheese on tope of noodles.
  9. Repeat steps 5-7 two more times, for a total of 9 layers.
  10. Put in oven for 60-65 minutes.
